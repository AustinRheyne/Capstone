My Take Away

Overall, I found that web development is really fun! Theres a massive community of people out there who love to help others learn the web and make it as simple as possible. I think it is really interesting how quickly someone can put together an entire website. Although this page is fairly small and simple, I still personally find it impressive that it only took about 4 hours to put the entire thing together. I believe that I will be furthering my web development education in the future in an attempt to become good enough at it to turn it into a full time job.
